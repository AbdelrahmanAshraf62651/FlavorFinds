Quick Tofu Stir-Fry

20 min
2–3 servings
4.6

Instructions

  1. 1Press tofu and cut into cubes; toss with cornstarch.
  2. 2Stir-fry tofu until crisp; set aside. Sauté vegetables.
  3. 3Combine soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and a touch of honey.
  4. 4Toss tofu and vegetables with sauce; cook 1–2 minutes.
Side Dish Vegetarian Quick

Ingredients

  • 300g firm tofu
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
