Classic Beef Lasagna

1 hr 30 min
6–8 servings
4.6

Instructions

  1. 1Prepare meat sauce with beef, onion, garlic, tomatoes, and herbs.
  2. 2Make béchamel (or use ricotta mixture) until smooth.
  3. 3Layer pasta, meat sauce, béchamel/ricotta, and cheese; repeat.
  4. 4Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 35–45 minutes until bubbly and golden.
Main Dish Italian Family-Style

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped; 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella + 1/2 cup parmesan
  • Béchamel or ricotta mixture
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper, herbs
